OUR PROCESS

Simple, Transparent, Reliable - From Pickup to Delivery

How It Works

Our streamlined four-step process ensures your package moves efficiently from point A to point B with complete transparency and security.

01

We Receive Your Package

The journey begins when you hand over your package to us. You can choose from multiple convenient options: schedule a pickup from your location, drop off at any of our service centers, or use our network of partner locations. Upon receipt, our team carefully inspects and documents your shipment, verifying contents, dimensions, and weight. We assign a unique tracking number that allows you to monitor your package throughout its journey. Your package is then photographed and logged into our advanced tracking system, ensuring complete accountability from the very first moment. All necessary documentation, including customs forms for international shipments, is completed and verified to prevent any delays.

02

We Process & Dispatch

Once received, your package enters our state-of-the-art sorting facility where advanced automation and skilled personnel work in harmony. Our intelligent routing system analyzes the destination, urgency, and package specifications to determine the optimal shipping method and route. Packages are sorted by destination zones, consolidated with other shipments heading in the same direction for efficiency, and prepared for dispatch. Security checks are performed, and appropriate packaging or reinforcement is added if needed. Your shipment is then loaded onto the designated transport vehicle ‚Äì whether truck, plane, or ship ‚Äì based on your chosen service level. Real-time updates are sent to you confirming dispatch, and your package officially begins its journey to its destination.

03

Package in Transit

Your package is now on the move! During transit, GPS-enabled tracking provides real-time location updates accessible through our website or mobile app. Each checkpoint ‚Äì whether a sorting facility, customs clearance, or transfer point ‚Äì generates an automatic scan and update. Our climate-controlled vehicles and specialized handling procedures ensure your package remains secure and protected throughout the journey, regardless of distance or weather conditions. For international shipments, our customs experts handle all clearance procedures, keeping you informed of any requirements or delays. You receive proactive notifications at key milestones, and our 24/7 support team is available should you have any questions during transit. Multiple quality checks ensure the package maintains its integrity until it reaches the final destination.

04

Delivered

The final step ‚Äì successful delivery! Our professional courier locates the delivery address and ensures the package reaches the intended recipient. We offer flexible delivery options including doorstep delivery, signature required, safe-drop locations, or hold for pickup at our service centers. Upon delivery, proof of delivery is captured with recipient signature, photo confirmation, and GPS coordinates, all accessible through your tracking portal. You receive an instant notification confirming successful delivery with all relevant details. For any delivery attempts that couldn't be completed, we immediately notify you and arrange a convenient redelivery or alternative pickup solution. Your feedback is requested to help us continuously improve our service, and our customer satisfaction team follows up to ensure you're completely satisfied with the delivery experience.
